Spotify Web Watcher

Usage

  1. Just play music on the Spotify Web Player
  2. The music you're listening to will be displayed on your stream

Configuration (1 minute)

  1. Open Spotify Web Player clicking here
  2. Log-in into Spotify
  3. The extension icon will turn green
  4. Click on the extension icon
  5. Copy the URL
  6. Use it on your streaming software (OBS/SLOBS/XSplit) as a "browser" source
  7. Done!
